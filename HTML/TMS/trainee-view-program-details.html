Program Objective
These are important rotations undertaken by pediatric residents. The junior general pediatric rotations are divided as follows:
  • 5 blocks in 1st year
  • 2 blocks in 2nd year Residents
do their rotation in Sultan Qaboos University Hospital, Royal Hospital and Al Nahdha Hospital. There are two general pediatric units in each hospital and each unit consists of a senior consultant with senior residents and 3 to 4 junior residents. The overall objective of this rotation is to prepare the resident to recognize and treat commonly encountered conditions as a general pediatrician. The residents will be supervised by the supervising faculty on a case by case basis.
Requirements
Perform a complete and accurate general pediatric history including birth(prenatal, natal and postnatal), growth, development, nutrition, immunization, treatment, family and social history
